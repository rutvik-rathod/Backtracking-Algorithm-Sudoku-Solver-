# Backtracking-Algorithm-Sudoku-Solver-
It is able to solve the sudoku using Backtrack Algorithms Approach

It takes 2D array as an Input and returns solved array
#INPUT:
7 8 0  | 4 0 0  | 1 2 0
6 0 0  | 0 7 5  | 0 0 9
0 0 0  | 6 0 1  | 0 7 8
------------------------
0 0 7  | 0 4 0  | 2 6 0
0 0 1  | 0 5 0  | 9 3 0
9 0 4  | 0 6 0  | 0 0 5
------------------------
0 7 0  | 3 0 0  | 0 1 2
1 2 0  | 0 0 7  | 4 0 0
0 4 9  | 2 0 6  | 0 0 7

The blank spaces are replaced by zeros

#OUTPUT:
7 8 5  | 4 3 9  | 1 2 6
6 1 2  | 8 7 5  | 3 4 9
4 9 3  | 6 2 1  | 5 7 8
------------------------
8 5 7  | 9 4 3  | 2 6 1
2 6 1  | 7 5 8  | 9 3 4
9 3 4  | 1 6 2  | 7 8 5
------------------------
5 7 8  | 3 9 4  | 6 1 2
1 2 6  | 5 8 7  | 4 9 3
3 4 9  | 2 1 6  | 8 5 7

NOTE: The dashes and the lines are only for visuals the won't be included in the arrays
